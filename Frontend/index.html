Delivery & Tracking

⚡ How fast can I expect my order?

Most components reach your doorstep within 42 minutes. We utilize a network of strategic micro-warehouses across the city to ensure your gear arrives while you're still in the flow.

🎯 What is the "60-Minute Guarantee"?

If your order doesn't arrive within 60 minutes, it's on us. We guarantee ultra-fast delivery; if we miss the mark, you get your money back and keep the components.

📍 Can I track my delivery in real-time?

Absolutely. Every SparkVolt order features Live GPS Tracking. Once your rider is dispatched, you can follow their progress on our interactive map directly through your dashboard.

Privacy Policy

Your data privacy and security is our top priority. Last Updated: April 2026

Your Privacy Matters

At SparkVolt, we are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices in detail.

1. Information We Collect 📋

To provide under-60-minute delivery and a seamless shopping experience, we collect the following types of information:

Personal Identifiers

Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.

Payment Information

Payment details are processed via secure, encrypted third-party gateways. We do not store your full credit card or CVV numbers on our servers.

Geolocation Data

Precise real-time location data to facilitate GPS tracking for deliveries and to identify the nearest micro-warehouse.

Technical Data

IP address, browser type, device information, and usage patterns on our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information 🎯

We utilize your data to maintain the speed and reliability of our services:

✓ Order Fulfillment

Processing transactions and ensuring your components arrive at the correct destination.

✓ Real-Time Tracking

Sharing your location with our delivery partners to optimize the delivery route.

✓ Customer Support

Responding to inquiries sent to support@sparkvolt.in or via our help desk.

✓ Service Optimization

Analyzing user behavior to improve our micro-warehouse inventory management and app performance.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure 🔄

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with:

🚚 Delivery Partners

Logistics personnel receive your name, address, and phone number to complete the delivery.

🔧 Service Providers

Third-party vendors who assist with payment processing, data analysis, and email communication.

⚖️ Legal Requirements

If required by law, we may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or protect the safety of our users.

4. Data Security 🔒

SparkVolt employs industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect data during transmission. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices ✋

You have the right to:

📝 Access & Update

Modify your account information at any time through the SparkVolt app or website.

📍 Location Permissions

Disable location tracking via your device settings (note: this may disable real-time tracking features).

🗑️ Data Deletion

Request the deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting our support team.

📧 Contact Us

For privacy-related requests, email privacy@sparkvolt.in

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies 🍪

We use cookies to remember your preferences and keep you logged in. You can manage cookie settings through your individual browser, but disabling them may limit your access to certain features of our platform.

7. Changes to This Policy 📢

SparkVolt reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy as our services evolve. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page.

For any privacy concerns or questions, contact us at privacy@sparkvolt.in
